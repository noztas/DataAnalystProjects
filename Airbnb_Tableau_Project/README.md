# Airbnb Seattle - Tableau Dashboards

Two interactive Tableau dashboards on Seattle Airbnb listings, built to show how price and host status play out across the city.

**Tools:** Tableau Public, Excel (data prep)
**Data:** [Airbnb Listings 2016 on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/alexanderfreberg/airbnb-listings-2016-dataset)

---

## Dashboards

**Price by neighborhood** - average price by ZIP code, room-type breakdown, accommodates vs price, and minimum-nights distribution, filterable by neighborhood and room type.

**Superhost vs non-Superhost** - ratings, review counts, and pricing compared between the two, filterable by room type, neighborhood, and group size.

## What I found

- Central neighborhoods like Capitol Hill and Queen Anne have the highest average prices
- Superhosts get higher ratings and more reviews than non-Superhosts
- Entire-home listings are priced higher but get fewer reviews
- Minimum-night requirements vary with listing type and host status
